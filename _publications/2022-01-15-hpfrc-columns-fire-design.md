This paper examined the fire behaviour of hybrid fibre reinforced high-performance concrete columns under uniaxial bending. Its contribution is to connect observed fire response with design-oriented recommendations for high-performance concrete columns where fibres, eccentricity, and elevated-temperature degradation interact.
