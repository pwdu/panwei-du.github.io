This paper examined shear-critical fibre-reinforced lightweight aggregate concrete beams after elevated-temperature exposure. Its contribution is to fill a gap in fire-affected shear behaviour, supporting safer assessment of lightweight concrete members where shear failure can be sudden and brittle.
