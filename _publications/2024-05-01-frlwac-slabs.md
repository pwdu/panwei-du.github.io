This paper combined experiments and modelling to study continuous fibre-reinforced lightweight aggregate concrete one-way slabs at ambient and elevated temperatures. Its novelty is the treatment of continuity and fire exposure together, supporting more realistic assessment of slab systems rather than isolated simply supported members.
