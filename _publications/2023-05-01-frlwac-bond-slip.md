This study investigated reinforcing-bar bond-slip behaviour in fibre reinforced lightweight aggregate concrete before and after elevated-temperature exposure. The novelty is its focus on a key local mechanism controlling member performance, providing data needed to model fire-affected lightweight concrete structures more reliably.
