This paper introduced transfer learning enhanced heat transfer neural operators for efficient thermal analysis in concrete structures. The novelty is the use of operator learning to predict full-field thermal responses across fire scenarios, reducing the dependence on repeated high-cost simulations while improving adaptability to new thermal conditions.
