I am a Research Fellow in Civil Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My research sits at the intersection of AI4Science, structural fire engineering, protective engineering, and sustainable cementitious materials.

I develop data-efficient computational models for structures exposed to extreme actions such as fire, elevated temperature, and blast. My work includes 15 peer-reviewed journal papers spanning neural operators, structural fire engineering, advanced concrete materials, and concrete/composite structural systems.

Research Interests
======

* Neural operators and scientific machine learning for structural engineering
* Heat transfer and fire resistance of concrete and composite structures
* Blast and impact response of fire-damaged structural members
* Fibre-reinforced, lightweight, and ultra-high-performance concrete
* Analytical and numerical modelling for practical structural design
* Sustainable cementitious materials and passive cooling technologies

Selected Highlights
======

* Developed transfer-learning enhanced heat transfer neural operators for efficient thermal analysis in concrete structures.
* Published 15 peer-reviewed journal papers on AI-assisted modelling, structural fire engineering, advanced concrete materials, and concrete/composite systems.
* Built analytical models for reinforced concrete and steel tubed-reinforced-concrete columns under eccentric compression and fire exposure.
* Contributed to experimental studies on bond, flexure, shear, and fire behaviour of fibre-reinforced lightweight aggregate concrete members.
* Collaborated on interdisciplinary work linking cementitious materials, heat transfer, and passive cooling.
